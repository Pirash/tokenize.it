tokenize.it

You can store here a short text, like a password, account number, or something else you don't want to put into an email or chat in clear. In return you will get a secret key that you, or someone you trust, can use to reveal the secret itself.
If you have a secret key, put it here to reveal the secret itself. Once revealed, it's forgotten from the server and cannot be brought back.
